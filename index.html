What is a Salmon Cookie?

Pat Salmon has always dreamed of creating the perfect cookie. This cookie not only would be sweet a savory, it would also be healthy for the environment! Hence Pat's Salmon Cookies were born! This delicious treat is perfect to eat alongside coffee or tea and is edible for both people and salmon! A portion of each salmon cookie sold is also donated to the Environment Health Association!
